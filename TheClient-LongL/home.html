Backstory in Short

Made up of 3 penguin conservation groups, the Purple Penguin Project is dedicated to hosting a festival held on World Penguin Day (April 25th) each year. This festival takes place in Maimi, FL, and its purpose is to bring awareness to, and raise money for, the conservation and care of all endangered species of penguins around the world. All of the money raised will go to any of the 3 penguin conservation groups that make up the Purple Penguin Project to aid them in their conservation efforts.
