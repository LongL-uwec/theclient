Purple Penguin Project

Morning
(9:30am - 12:00pm)

Aziz Ansari (9:30am - 10:30am)

Aziz Ismail Ansari is an actor, comedian, writer, director and producer. He is known widely for his role in "Parks and Recreation" and as creater and star of the Netflix series "Master of None". With 5 comedy specials already under his belt, Ansari's 6th comedy special to be performed at the Purple Penguin Project event will be one for the books.

Dua Lipa (11:00am - 12:00am)

Dua Lipa is a singer/songwriter known for her signature disco-pop sound. Some of her more popular songs include "New Rules", "One Kiss" (with Calvin Harris), "Don't Start Now", "Levitating" (featuring DaBaby), and "Break My Heart". She will be performing songs from her 2 albums, some hit singles, and she may even debut something new.

Afternoon
(1:00pm - 5:00pm)

Dave Chappelle (1:00pm - 2:00pm)

David Khari Webber Chappelle is a stand-up comedian and actor known for his satirical comedy sketch series "Chappelle's Show". He has released several comedy specials with Netflix and has appeared in several films during his career. Chappelle will be performing and using content from his Grammy Award-Winning comedy albums in addition to some new, yet to be seen, content.

Carrie Underwood (2:30pm - 3:30pm)

Carrie Marie Underwood is a country singer who rose to prominence after winning the fourth season of American Idol. Some of her more popular releases include "Jesus, Take the Wheel", "Before He Cheats", "Cowboy Casanova", "Blown Away", and "Something in the Water". Underwood will be performing several songs from her 6 country albums, and she may perform something from her 2021 gospel collection.

Florida Georgia Line (4:00pm - 5:00pm)

Florida Georgia Line is a country music duo founded by Tyler Hubbard and Brian Kelley, both of whom are singer/songwriters. They have many hits including "H.O.L.Y.", "Get Your Shine On", and "This Is How We Roll", but their most popular song to date is their debut single "Cruise". Florida Georgia Line will be performing these hits among several others from their 5 albums.

Evening
(6:00pm - 10:00pm)

Kevin Hart (6:00pm - 7:00pm)

Kevin Darnell Hart is a comedian and actor known for his comedy albums and his roles in films such as "Think Like a Man" and "Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle" among many others. Hart will be debuting a brand new comedy album at the Purple Penguin Project event in addition to performing some of his most-loved content from his other 5 comedy albums.

Doja Cat (7:30pm - 8:30pm)

Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, known professionally as Doja Cat, is a rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. Some of her more popular songs include "Say So", "Kiss Me More", "Need to Know", and "Woman". Doja Cat will be performing these hits among others from her 2 most recent albums, and she may debut 1 or 2 new songs to be released with her up-and-coming album.

The Chainsmokers (9:00pm - 10:00pm)

The Chainsmokers are an electronic DJ and production duo consisting of Alexander "Alex" Pall and Andrew "Drew" Taggart. They are known for their songs "Roses", "Don't Let Me Down" (featuring Daya), and "Closer" (featuring Halsey) among others. The Chainsmokers will be putting on an unforgettable performance of their top hits from all 4 albums.
