Purple Penguin Project

Current Donations and Goal

Though the Purple Penguin Project receives most of its donations/money through the event, smaller donations are accepted every day. Current donations rest at $7,539.50. With the Purple Penguin Project event in mind, the donation goal to reach by April 25, 2023 is $5,000,000. Every penny counts!

Where does my donation go?

All donations received by the Purple Penguin Project are distributed between the 3 conservation groups that formed the Purple Penguin Project: Global Penguin Society, Penguins International, and World Wildlife Fund. All 3 of these conservation groups take different approaches toward conserving and helping penguins, but they all have the same mission. Save the Penguins.
